The Ritual

How the oath is kept daily.

Neither oil needs a routine built around it — just a few honest habits.

Skin

Warm three drops between your palms and press into damp skin — never rub, just press.

Hair

Work blackseed oil through the ends twice a week, an hour before washing out.

Kitchen

Finish, don't fry. Drizzle avocado oil over the plate right before it reaches the table.

Wellness

One teaspoon of blackseed, taken plain, the way it's been taken for generations.
